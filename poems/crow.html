Where are my crow?

"caw"
I call out,
No reply.

"caw!"
I call out,
No reply.

In my minds eye,
things have gone awry,
my murder is not nearby.

"CAW!"

My mother used to sigh,
"a crow never forgets a face,
look up, look them in the eye,
anywhere you go,
you will see a crow."

"NO!" I decry!
"Mother it was all a lie!"
"CAAAAAAW!"
