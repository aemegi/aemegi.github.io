The Tinkerer and The Cart

The toy,
It spins,
It twirls,
You can stack it,
You can bend it,
You can pull it apart,
and put it back together.

The man,
He was so smart,
He thought:
"Why this toy is a work of art,
I want this toy to be my cart,
Then I can use it to carry all my art."

He tried,
and tried,
but this toy was not a cart,
this toy was it's own work of art.
